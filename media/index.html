# Talk to me

If you would like my opinion on a topic of skeptical interest, please feel free to contact me. I'm happy to give my opinion as Media Spokesperson for the NZ Skeptics (opens new window), Vice President of Humanist NZ (opens new window) or Chair of the Society for Science Based Healthcare (opens new window).
